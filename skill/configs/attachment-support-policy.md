# Attachment Support Policy

## Fixed States

```text
无附件 / 解析成功 / 格式不支持 / 解析失败
```

## General Guidance

- Common candidate formats: `PNG`, `JPG`, `PDF`, `DOCX`, `XLSX`, `TXT`.
- Do not promise support for a format without checking the selected model.
- If parsing fails, ask the customer to provide a supported format or paste the relevant text.
- Do not invent content from unreadable attachments.
